Why Study at George Brown School of Design?

George Brown Polytechnic offers students a practical and career-focused design education in the heart of Toronto. Our programs combine creative thinking, technical development, and hands-on learning to help students build industry-ready portfolios.

Students work on real-world design challenges while developing skills in branding, digital media, typography, visual systems, and emerging creative technologies. The School of Design supports growth, experimentation, and a strong professional mindset.
